Create A Circle, Spin, or Loop

A Circle is a group of friends who can gather for different events. It can have many Spins or Loops within it.

A Spin is a single meetup. It can be a part of a Circle, and it can be converted into a Loop.

A Loop is a recurring meetup. It can be part of a Circle, and it can be converted into a Spin.
